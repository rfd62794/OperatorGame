# NARRATIVE: The Hoot-and-Holler Protocol

> **"You aren't just managing slimes; you are managing the fallout of a planet that is starting to notice you."**

## 1. The "Hoot-and-Holler" Interaction
The "Hoot-and-Holler" is the primary interaction loop between the Astronaut (User) and the planetary environment. It represents the frantic, imperfect nature of commanding biological entities in a high-stress, resonant environment.

### The Mechanical Loop
1. **The Call**: Each click-to-action is a "Hoot"—a pulse of energy from the ship.
2. **The Response**: Slimes "Holler" back through the AAR narrative, reflecting their success or failure based on their Soft Stats (Mind/Tenacity).

## 2. Operator vs. Planet (The Tension)
The core narrative tension is the **Mission Paradox**:
- **The Astronaut** wants rapid growth, rare resources (GEL/SCRAP), and a way home.
- **The Planet** responds to this activity with **Resonance Resistance** (Startled Level).

### Startled Level: The Global Stress Counter
The `startled_level` is more than a UI bar; it is the planet's heartbeat.
- **High Stress**: When the `startled_level` is high, the planet’s frequencies become "jagged."
- **Frantic Commands**: High stress forces the Astronaut to issue "Frantic Commands," which have a higher chance of triggering a **Startled Reaction** in slimes with low Tenacity (TEN).
- **Hooting Astronaut**: If the Astronaut pushes too hard (too many clicks/missions in a short time), the Startled Level spikes, increasing the floor for permanent death (KIA).

## 3. The Elder's Wisdom
The Elder (Node 0) acts as the bridge. Through the **Mentorship System**, the Elder provides "Wisdom Insights" that help the Astronaut navigate the tension.
- **Blessing System**: Meditating or offering resources to the Elder can temporarily lower the Startled Level or provide "Structural Harmony" for the ship.

## 4. The Void Glitch (Narrative Anomaly)
The "Void Glitch" is the ultimate narrative mystery. It is a corruption of the planetary frequency—a "White Light" that suggests the Astronaut may be becoming part of the planet itself rather than escaping it.
- **Discovery**: Only triggered when a slime's genome reaches the "Universal Constant" state (Tier 8).
- **AAR Injection**: Void slimes generate unique narrative logs that speak in fragmented, ancient planetary code.
