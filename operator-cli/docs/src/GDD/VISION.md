# VISION: The Unity of Purpose

> **"The ship didn't just crash; it was pulled. The local 'Cultures' aren't just elements; they are resonant frequencies of the planet's core."**

## 1. The Core Mission
The Astronaut's arrival on the planet was not a catastrophic accident, but a **Resonance Attraction**. The gravitational anomaly that brought the ship down was a "Pull Event" (Resonant Frequency 432.1Hz) triggered by the planet's core.

The mission is no longer just survival; it is **Ascension**. By breeding slimes that resonate with the planet's nine chromatic frequencies, the Astronaut builds a "Key" to slip the gravity well.

## 2. The Elder's Meadow (Node 0)
The **Elder's Meadow** is the historical landing site and the heart of the "Union." It serves as the player's primary Safe Haven.

### Mechanical Anchor: +2 Resonance Aura
Node 0 provides a global **Resonance Aura** that stabilizes the biological frequencies of slimes within the ship.
- **Garden Stability**: Slimes in the Elder's Meadow recover from "Startled" status 20% faster.
- **Repair Efficiency**: The presence of the Elder provides a +2 modifier to all Soft Stat (MND/TEN) checks performed at this location.

## 3. The Void Culture (The Glitch)
The **Void Culture** is the "Glitched" 4th primary culture. It does not exist as a base spawn but must be "discovered" through high-tier mutation triggers.

### The White Light Mechanics
Void is the synthesis of the complete chromatic spectrum (Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary colors).
- **Dominance**: Void slimes ignore all RPS (Rock-Paper-Scissors) disadvantages. They are the "Universal Constant."
- **Dominant Dominance**: Breedings involving a Void parent have a higher mutation floor (15%+).
- **Ascension Progress**: Achieving a Tier 8 Void Slime is the prerequisite for the final "White Light" fusion event.

Every biological entity is a walk-on part in the planet's symphony.
- **Resonant Frequencies**:
