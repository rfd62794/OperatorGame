# Culture & Genetic Expressions

The Operator ecosystem diverges from standard numerical classes by deriving stats and specializations from biological origins. These **Cultures** correspond with specific platonic solids, colours, and frequencies.

## The Archetypes and Stat Correlations

Under ADR-037, **Slimes are the Operators**. Their base capabilities (Strength, Agility, Intelligence) are biologically rooted in their cultural archetype. Gear acts as a "Resonance Dampener" rather than standard armour, stabilizing the slime's form for specific mission parameters.

### Ember (Red / Tetrahedron)
* **Archetype**: Assault / Breacher
* **Core Frequency**: 256.0 Hz
* **Physical Profile**: High Base Strength. Embers hit hard and thrive in direct-conflict missions.

### Gale (Yellow / Octahedron)
* **Archetype**: Speed / Infiltrator
* **Core Frequency**: 288.0 Hz
* **Physical Profile**: High Base Agility. Gales are evasive, swift, and essential for stealth or time-critical operations.

### Tide (Blue / Icosahedron)
* **Archetype**: Logic / Analyst
* **Core Frequency**: 320.0 Hz
* **Physical Profile**: High Base Intelligence. Tides excel at complex data extraction, cyber warfare, and analytical ops.

*(Note: Hybrid and synthesized slimes inherit and blend these properties across the color wheel, creating specialized variants suitable for cross-disciplinary contracts).*
