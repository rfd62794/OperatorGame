# The Shepherd's Guide

> **Welcome, Astronaut.** You are the bridge between the screaming chaos of your own making and the resonant harmony of an alien world. This guide will help you understand the frequencies, shapes, and songs that govern the planet you now call home.

## Overview

OPERATOR rpgCore is not just a game—it's a harmonic instrument where every interaction creates music, every shape is a visible sound, and every culture sings its own song. As the Shepherd, you are the conductor of this planetary orchestra, learning to harmonize with the ancient Elder and the nine chromatic cultures that form the planet's living web.

## The Core Philosophy

### Frequency-Driven Design

Everything in OPERATOR is driven by frequency, not arbitrary numbers:

- **Cultures** resonate at specific frequencies from the Solfeggio scale
- **Shapes** are cymatic patterns made visible by sound
- **Colors** represent the emotional tone of each frequency
- **Interactions** create harmonic intervals rather than random outcomes

### The Healing Scale

We use the Solfeggio frequencies (174 Hz to 963 Hz) because they are mathematically consistent with the harmonic series and have been shown to have healing effects on human physiology. Each culture's frequency is carefully chosen to create a balanced, harmonious ecosystem.

### Cymatic Rendering

The visual system renders sound waves as geometric shapes. When you see a slime, you're actually seeing the physical manifestation of its frequency. Higher frequencies create complex fractal patterns, while lower frequencies create grounded, solid forms.
