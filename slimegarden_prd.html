# SlimeGarden Mobile Game UI Design PRD ## Game Concept SlimeGarden is a Mafia Wars-style mobile game where you manage a crew of colorful slimes, dispatch them on quests, and review results when they return. Dark gritty mafia aesthetic with vibrant, gooey slime elements. ## Design Style - **Theme:** Dark industrial + colorful slimes (vibrant reds, blues, yellows, oranges, purples, greens). - **Tone:** Corpo-speak (mission briefings), AAR narrative (after-action reports), slight humor. - **Visual Hierarchy:** Text-heavy, stat-focused, clear call-to-action buttons. - **Target Device:** Mobile portrait (412dp wide). ## UI Structure: 4 Tabs 1. **ROSTER:** Collection (grid of slime cards with stats/level) and Breeding (pair manager, incubator). 2. **MISSIONS:** Active (in-progress/complete tracking) and Quest Board (available missions with stat requirements). 3. **MAP:** Zones (ring-based structure with affinity bonuses and unlock gates). 4. **LOGS:** Mission History (AAR details) and Culture History (timeline of discovered slime types). ## Visual Guidelines - **Backgrounds:** #0f0f14, #1a1a22 - **Culture Colors:** Ember (Red: #ff4444), Tide (Blue: #4488ff), Gale (Yellow: #ffdd44), Orange (#ff8844), Purple (#dd44ff), Green (#44dd88). - **Typography:** Bold headers (14-16pt), Monospace stats (10pt). - **Components:** Rectangular cards with subtle borders, 44dp min touch height for buttons.
